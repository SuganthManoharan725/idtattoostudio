About Us

Welcome to ID Tattoo Studio: Where Art Meets Skin

At ID Tattoo Studio, located in the vibrant heart of Chennai, we believe that every tattoo tells a unique story. Established by the passionate artist Vicky, our studio is more than just a place to get inked – it's a sanctuary where creativity flourishes and individuality thrives.

Our studio is a haven where creativity knows no bounds and individuality is celebrated. With a legacy of over a decade and a portfolio boasting 1000+ satisfied clients, including esteemed celebrities, we are proud to offer unparalleled expertise and craftsmanship in every design.

Whether you're seeking a bold statement piece or a subtle, intricate design, our talented team is dedicated to bringing your vision to life. Step into our studio and experience the difference – where passion, professionalism, and creativity converge to make your tattoo dreams a reality.

Owner

Meet Vicky: Master Tattoo Artist and Mentor

With a decade of experience and a portfolio adorned with over 1000 satisfied clients, Vicky stands as a beacon of excellence in the world of tattoo artistry. Renowned for his meticulous attention to detail and unwavering dedication to his craft, Vicky has etched his mark on the skin of numerous celebrities and discerning clients alike.

Beyond creating stunning works of art, Vicky is committed to nurturing the next generation of tattoo artists. Through personalized training programs and mentorship, he shares his wealth of knowledge and expertise, empowering aspiring artists to unlock their full potential and embark on their own creative journeys.
